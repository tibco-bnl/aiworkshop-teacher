# Taipei Logic Inc. - Corporate Information

## Company Overview

**Taipei Logic Inc.** is a fabless semiconductor company based in Taipei, Taiwan. The company designs high-performance, low-power logic chips (CPUs, GPUs, and APUs) for the edge computing, automotive, and consumer electronics markets. Taipei Logic is a key player in the Taiwanese "Silicon Shield" ecosystem.

## Strategy & Vision

### Mission Statement
To design the intelligent chips that power the next generation of connected devices, driving innovation from the data center to the edge.

### Strategic Vision 2025-2030
Our "Edge Intelligence" strategy is built on three pillars:

**1. AI-at-the-Edge Leadership**
- Designing high-efficiency Neural Processing Units (NPUs) for on-device AI.
- Creating low-power SoCs (System-on-a-Chip) for smart cameras, drones, and IoT.
- Leading in advanced packaging (chiplet) technology.

**2. Automotive & Mobility**
- Developing ASIL-certified processors for ADAS (Advanced Driver-Assistance Systems).
- Designing chips for next-generation in-vehicle infotainment (IVI) systems.
- Low-latency processors for V2X (Vehicle-to-Everything) communication.

**3. High-Performance Consumer Tech**
- Custom-designed APUs for high-end laptops, tablets, and handheld gaming.
- Energy-efficient processors for AR/VR and mixed-reality headsets.

### Key Innovation Areas
- **Neural Processing Unit (NPU) Design**
- **Advanced Packaging (Chiplet & 3D Stacking)**
- **Low-Power Architecture**
- **Automotive-Grade (ASIL) Processors**

## Technology Infrastructure & Computing Requirements

### High-Performance Computing Architecture
As a fabless design house, Taipei Logic's entire "factory" is its data center, used for chip design, simulation, and verification.

**AI & Machine Learning Infrastructure:**
- **High-Core-Count CPUs:** Massive clusters for Electronic Design Automation (EDA) simulation, logic verification, and place-and-route.
- **High-Memory GPUs:** Used for AI-driven chip design (e.g., floorplanning) and training AI models that will be burned into their NPUs.
- **High-Speed Memory & Storage:** Required for handling massive chip design files and simulation datasets.

**Cloud & Edge Computing:**
- **Server-Grade Processors:** Used for internal development and client simulation environments.
- **FPGA Prototyping:** Requires high-end FPGAs and related compute to emulate chip designs before tape-out.

### Strategic Technology Partnerships
- **TSMC:** Primary foundry partner for advanced-node manufacturing (5nm, 3nm).
- **ASE Group:** Partner for advanced packaging and testing.
- **Cadence & Synopsys:** Key suppliers and partners for EDA tools.
- **ARM:** Architectural licensee for CPU and GPU cores.
