# Dragon Chip Corp. - Corporate Information

## Company Overview

**Dragon Chip Corp.** is a rapidly growing fabless semiconductor company headquartered in Shenzhen, China. The company designs high-performance System-on-a-Chip (SoC) solutions, specializing in AI-powered processors for smart devices, consumer electronics, and video surveillance. Dragon Chip is a key player in China's "Made in China 2025" initiative.

## Strategy & Vision

### Mission Statement
To power the future of intelligent devices by designing China's most advanced and efficient AI-native chips, driving domestic innovation and technological self-reliance.

### Strategic Vision 2025-2030
Our "Intelligent Core" (智能核心) strategy is focused on:

**1. AIoT & Smart Home Dominance**
- Designing high-performance, low-cost SoCs for smart speakers, displays, and home robots.
- Leading the market in AI-processors for smart security and surveillance cameras.
- Developing ultra-low-power chips for battery-operated IoT devices.

**2. Mobile & Consumer Electronics**
- Designing competitive application processors (APs) for mid-range smartphones and tablets.
- Creating high-performance chips for AR glasses and consumer drones.
- Expanding into Wi-Fi 7 and 5G connectivity chipsets.

**3. Technological Self-Reliance**
- Building a domestic IP core portfolio (CPU, GPU, NPU) to reduce reliance on foreign licensing.
- Partnering with domestic foundries (SMIC, Hua Hong) to secure production capacity.
- Leading national standards for AI and IoT chip design.

### Key Innovation Areas
- **Neural Processing Unit (NPU) for Video**
- **Low-Power SoC Architecture**
- **RISC-V Core Development**
- **AI-Based Image Signal Processing (ISP)**

## Technology Infrastructure & Computing Requirements

### High-Performance Computing Architecture
As a major fabless design house, Dragon Chip requires massive data centers for Electronic Design Automation (EDA), simulation, and verification.

**AI & Machine Learning Infrastructure:**
- **High-Core-Count CPUs:** The workhorse for logic simulation, verification, and physical design (place-and-route).
- **High-Memory GPUs:** Used to train AI models that are later implemented in their NPU hardware.
- **High-Speed Memory & Storage:** Required for handling the extremely large design files and simulation datasets typical in advanced-node chip design.

**Cloud & Edge Computing:**
- **Server-Grade Processors:** Building out a private cloud for their EDA workloads.
- **FPGA Prototyping:** Large-scale FPGA farms to emulate and validate chip designs before tape-out.

### Strategic Technology Partnerships
- **SMIC (Semiconductor Manufacturing International Corp):** Primary domestic foundry partner.
- **Huawei (HiSilicon):** Strategic collaboration and technology sharing.
- **Tsinghua University:** Joint R&D lab for next-generation AI and RISC-V architectures.
- **Baidu:** Partnership on "Kunlun" AI chip development and cloud-to-edge AI.
