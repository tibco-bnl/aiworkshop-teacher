# Parisian Tech Group - Corporate Information

## Company Overview

**Parisian Tech Group** is a leading French technology conglomerate headquartered in Paris, France. Founded in 2017, PTG operates across multiple high-technology sectors including artificial intelligence, fintech, smart city infrastructure, and telecommunications. The company represents the convergence of French engineering excellence with Silicon Valley innovation methodologies.

**Corporate Details:**
- **Headquarters:** 45 Avenue des Champs-Élysées, 8th Arrondissement, Paris, France
- **Contact:** corporate@parisiantech.fr | +33 1 42 68 53 00
- **Founded:** 2017
- **Industry:** Technology Conglomerate & Digital Innovation
- **Employee Count:** 2,847 (as of 2025)
- **Legal Structure:** Société par Actions Simplifiée (SAS)
- **Stock Exchange:** Euronext Paris (Ticker: PTG)

## Strategy & Vision

### Mission Statement
To position France as a global leader in next-generation technologies by building scalable, innovative solutions that enhance digital transformation across industries while preserving European digital sovereignty.

### Strategic Vision 2025-2030
Our "Tech Souveraineté" strategic framework encompasses five strategic pillars:

**1. Digital Sovereignty Leadership**
- Developing European alternatives to US-dominated tech platforms
- Building secure, GDPR-compliant cloud infrastructure solutions
- Advancing French AI capabilities through sovereign data processing

**2. Smart City Innovation Hub**
- Creating integrated IoT ecosystems for French metropolitan areas
- Developing sustainable urban technology solutions
- Leading European smart mobility and transportation initiatives

**3. Fintech & Digital Banking Revolution**
- Building next-generation payment processing systems
- Developing blockchain-based financial services
- Creating AI-driven risk management platforms

**4. Telecom Infrastructure Modernization**
- Advancing 5G and future 6G network technologies
- Developing edge computing solutions for telecom operators
- Building quantum-secure communication systems

**5. European Technology Alliance**
- Strengthening partnerships with German and Dutch tech companies
- Leading EU technology standardization initiatives
- Promoting "Made in Europe" technology solutions

### Key Innovation Areas
- **Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning** - French-language AI models
- **Quantum Computing Applications** - Financial modeling and cryptography
- **Edge Computing Platforms** - Industrial IoT and smart city deployments
- **Cybersecurity Solutions** - European data protection and digital sovereignty
