# Britannic Circuits Ltd. - Corporate Information

## Company Overview

**Britannic Circuits Ltd.** is a leading UK-based designer and manufacturer of high-performance radio frequency (RF) systems and compound semiconductors. Headquartered in London with R&D facilities in Cambridge, the company provides critical components for the telecommunications, defense, and aerospace markets.

## Strategy & Vision

### Mission Statement
To connect and protect the world by engineering the most advanced radio frequency and compound semiconductor solutions for mission-critical applications.

### Strategic Vision 2025-2030
Our "Spectrum Dominance" strategy focuses on:

**1. 5G & 6G Leadership**
- Designing high-efficiency GaN (Gallium Nitride) power amplifiers for 5G base stations.
- Researching terahertz (THz) frequency technologies for future 6G networks.
- Developing advanced RF filters and front-end modules.

**2. Aerospace & Defense**
- Building next-generation radar systems (AESA) for defense applications.
- Providing radiation-hardened components for satellites and space exploration.
- Secure communication systems for military and government clients.

**3. Advanced Materials R&D**
- Expanding our UK-based compound semiconductor (GaN, GaAs) fabrication.
- Researching new materials like SiC (Silicon Carbide) for power electronics.
- Leading UK efforts in quantum sensing technologies.

### Key Innovation Areas
- **Gallium Nitride (GaN) RF Amplifiers**
- **AESA Radar Systems**
- **Satellite (SatCom) RF Modules**
- **Compound Semiconductor Fabrication**

## Technology Infrastructure & Computing Requirements

### High-Performance Computing Architecture
Britannic Circuits relies on HPC for complex electromagnetic (EM) simulation, material science modeling, and chip design.

**AI & Machine Learning Infrastructure:**
- **High-Core-Count CPUs:** Running complex RF and EM simulations (e.g., Ansys HFSS) which are CPU-intensive.
- **High-Memory GPUs:** Used for AI-driven signal processing R&D and accelerating material science simulations.
- **High-Speed Memory:** Essential for handling the large datasets generated by RF simulations.

**Cloud & Edge Computing:**
- **Server-Grade Processors:** Internal servers for managing fab data and design IP.
- **FPGA Prototyping:** Prototyping new radar and communication controller logic.
- **Security Processors:** Hardware-based encryption is critical for their defense-related IP.

### Strategic Technology Partnerships
- **BAE Systems:** Key partner for defense systems integration.
- **Airbus:** Supplier for satellite communication modules.
- **ARM:** Partner in the Cambridge "Silicon Fen" ecosystem, collaboration on IoT.
- **University of Cambridge:** Joint R&D on compound semiconductors.
