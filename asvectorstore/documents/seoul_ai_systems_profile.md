# Seoul AI Systems - Corporate Information

## Company Overview

**Seoul AI Systems (SAIS)** is a leading South Korean technology company specializing in applied artificial intelligence and large-scale data processing. Based in the vibrant Gangnam district, SAIS develops AI-driven solutions for e-commerce, finance, and media, with a focus on natural language processing (NLP) and predictive analytics.

## Strategy & Vision

### Mission Statement
To unlock the power of data by building scalable, ethical, and cutting-edge AI solutions that drive business transformation and enhance digital experiences.

### Strategic Vision 2025-2030
Our "AI Everywhere" strategy is guided by three core pillars:

**1. NLP & Language Model Leadership**
- Developing best-in-class Korean-language AI models (LLMs).
- Building AI-driven translation, content generation, and chatbot solutions.
- Creating generative AI platforms for media and entertainment.

**2. Predictive Analytics & E-commerce**
- AI-powered recommendation engines and personalization platforms.
- Developing fraud detection and risk management models for fintech.
- AI for supply chain optimization and demand forecasting.

**3. AI Ethics & Governance**
- Pioneering "Explainable AI" (XAI) frameworks.
- Building robust data privacy and anonymization tools.
- Leading Korean corporate standards for ethical AI deployment.

### Key Innovation Areas
- **Large Language Models (LLMs) - Korean**
- **Generative AI (Video & Audio)**
- **AI-Driven Fraud Detection**
- **Explainable AI (XAI) Platforms**
